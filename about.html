Our Story

The story behind Toasty Appliance Repair started off with a couple teenagers. Someone made a joke about a fake brand called Toasty Toaster Ovens. Aiden Myers turned the joke into a appliance repair company. With the the person that thought of the toaster oven knowing, they used to be apart of Toasty Appliance Repair. Aiden brought up a group of members to help with the project and it brought the company to where it is now. With it being shut off for a period of time to now being brought back. The team plans to have it officially opened by sometime in 2028.
